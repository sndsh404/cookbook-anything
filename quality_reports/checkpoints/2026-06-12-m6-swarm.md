# Checkpoint: M6 acquisition/verification swarm

Date: 2026-06-12 (session 2)

## The numbers

3 reader workers cover a 6-chapter PDF book in parallel (6/6 chapters,
adjacent assignments overlap by one chapter); 9 claims admitted, every one
span-backed; 2 planted unsourced findings (a fake span id and a fabricated
sentence on a real span) rejected; 0 unsourced facts in the model after
admission. Independent support raises a claim's confidence (0.6 -> 0.7 with
a second span); a span-backed contradiction mints a second claim and an
event in runs.jsonl while the original stays active.

## What shipped

- `runner/swarm/swarm.ts`: the two primitives from OpenAI's swarm and
  nothing else. Agent (name + instructions + function), handoff (a result
  naming the next agent), one shared contextVariables object, plus a
  parallel fan-out where worker context updates merge (arrays concatenate).
  Stateless, small, no LLM in the loop yet by design.
- `runner/swarm/book_swarm.ts`: librarian coordinator -> reader workers ->
  archivist for acquisition; verify-coordinator -> verifier workers for
  verification. Workers only read; they cannot touch model.json.
- `core/ca-cli/src/admit.rs` + `ca admit`: the single door into the model.
  Re-checks every finding (span exists, text verbatim in span), groups
  agreement (confidence 0.6 + 0.15 per extra worker, cap 0.95), support
  appends spans (+0.1), contradiction records and never overwrites,
  rejects the rest, validates before saving, logs events to runs.jsonl.
- `core/ca-extract` per-page PDF spans (book.pdf#p3) so chapter workers
  own real spans; single-page PDFs keep their old locator.

## Decisions

- Studied swarm-main (OpenAI): adopted Agent + handoff + context_variables
  verbatim as the shape; rejected MiroFish's crowd-simulation framing per
  instruction. Our workers are deterministic functions for now; the
  primitives do not care, and the firewall holds regardless of who runs.
- Worker claim filters are candidates only. Trust lives in `ca admit`,
  which re-verifies everything; a malicious or sloppy worker can at worst
  waste its own time.
- Disagreement is two live claims plus a recorded event, not a vote.
