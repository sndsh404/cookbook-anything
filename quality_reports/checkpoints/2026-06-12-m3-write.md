# Checkpoint: M3 plan + write with the leash on

Date: 2026-06-12 (session 1)

## The numbers

Chapter prerequisite graph acyclic (prereqs may only point backwards by
construction; forward deps counted and dropped honestly). Claim coverage
100% on the full llmwiki draft (gate >= 95). Shipped prose: 0 banned words,
0 em dashes. The planted unsupported sentence ("The scheduler rebalances
shards every night...") is flagged by the verifier. Prose lints catch 10/10
planted slop violations.

## What shipped

- `ca plan` (core/ca-cli/src/plan.rs): clusters ordered by average
  dependency position; figure plan per chapter with a written why; page-one
  figure assigned before a word is written.
- `ca write` (write.rs): drafts strictly from the model with {{c:NNNN}}
  markers. Two claim kinds: doc claims (verbatim source sentences) and
  derived claims minted from graph structure with the spans of the nodes
  they describe (id prefix c:w, regenerated each run, never accumulated).
  Emits TL;DR, CARS intro, chapters with figures and captions, cookbook
  steps marked [unverified], glossary, claims appendix, unverified appendix,
  coverage.json. --ship strips markers.
- `ca verify` (verify.rs): adversarial both ways. Every marker resolves;
  doc-claim text must appear verbatim in its cited span; factual-looking
  sentences WITHOUT a marker are flagged, not fixed.
- `figlib/lint_prose.py`: banned vocabulary (multi-word phrases match across
  line wraps), em dashes, caption presence, long sentences, topic-sentence
  fragments. `figlib/seeded_defects/prose_slop.md` is the twin corpus.

## Bugs the gate caught before they shipped

1. The verifier's sentence splitter divorced sentences from their own
   markers (split on ". " made each marker its own chunk), flagging every
   marked sentence. Markers now bind to the last sentence of the segment
   they follow.
2. "it's important to note" planted across a line wrap was invisible to a
   literal-space regex; banned phrases now match \s+.
3. Em dashes leaked into the body through quoted docstrings; derived-claim
   quotes (not verbatim-checked) now get the voice profile applied.

## Decisions

- Lint scope is authored prose only (before ## Glossary); glossary and
  appendices quote source material verbatim as evidence.
- The LLM humanize-auditor remains the unimplemented judgment layer on top
  of these deterministic lints, same posture as the figure critic (M2).
